Who benefits from COMET — and how.

COMET provides a shared vocabulary that transforms friction into flow. Each stakeholder group below has five documented use cases with quantified benefits drawn from published benchmarks, regulatory impact assessments, and pilot programme results. Click any card to explore.

Ecosystem View

How data flows through COMET.

COMET acts as a semantic backbone between data producers and data consumers. Producers encode their process and emissions data into the seven-layer ontology stack; downstream consumers receive structured, verifiable, comparable outputs without bespoke integrations.
